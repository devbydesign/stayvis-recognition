What Makes Performance Recognition Effective

Strategic implementation ensures maximum motivational impact and lasting behavioral change.

Immediate Recognition

Recognize exceptional performance within 7 days of achievement. Immediate acknowledgment has exponentially more impact than delayed recognition and reinforces positive behaviors effectively.

Specific Achievement Focus

Link recognition directly to specific accomplishments and behaviors. Clear connections between performance and rewards help employees understand exactly what excellence looks like in your organization.

Visible Celebration

Make recognition public when appropriate. Visible celebration not only honors the individual but also demonstrates to all employees that exceptional performance is noticed, valued, and rewarded.
