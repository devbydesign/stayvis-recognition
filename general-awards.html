Building Award Programs That Work

Proven strategies that ensure your formal recognition program creates meaningful impact and drives lasting behavior change.

Clear Criteria

Establish transparent, measurable criteria for each award category. When employees understand what excellence looks like, they can strive to achieve it. Clear criteria also ensure fair and consistent recognition across your organization.

Regular Ceremonies

Schedule formal recognition events that create anticipation and celebration. Monthly, quarterly, or annual ceremonies give awards weight and significance while providing opportunities for organization-wide recognition and inspiration.

Peer Nomination

Include peer nominations in your award process to ensure diverse perspectives and increased buy-in. When employees can nominate colleagues, it creates a more inclusive process and stronger team engagement with the program.
